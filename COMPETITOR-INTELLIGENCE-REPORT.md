# Competitor Intelligence Report — Živina Kimchi

**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Market:** Czech Republic — Fermented Foods / Kimchi
**Subject:** zivina.cz competitive landscape

---

## Executive Summary

The Czech kimchi market has 5–6 active players, all small-to-mid-sized Czech producers. **Beavia is the retail distribution leader** (Tesco, Albert, Billa, Kaufland, Globus) with a new TV campaign (Jan 2026). **Živina has the strongest digital presence** — 45K Instagram followers, 26.7K Facebook likes, 300+ retail stores, and the only structured multi-channel digital campaign (YouTube + Meta, launched Nov 2025). The rest rely almost entirely on organic social and marketplace presence. **The paid advertising space for kimchi in CZ is wide open.**

## Competitor Profiles

### 1. Beavia (ilovekimchi.cz / beavia.com)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Company** | I love Hummus, s.r.o. (Všechlapy, Benešov) |
| **Founded** | 2011 (hummus), kimchi added 2017 |
| **Products** | Kimchi (classic, mild, spicy, extra spicy), BIO kimchi, fermented beets, sauerkraut, miso |
| **Pricing** | Kimchi 350g: 169 Kč; Extra spicy 350g: 179 Kč; Yumchi 200g: 149 Kč |
| **Distribution** | D2C eshop + **major retail**: Tesco, Albert, Billa, Kaufland, Globus, Metro/Makro |
| **Markets** | CZ, DE, AT, PL, HU, SK |
| **Social** | @beavia_cz (Instagram), Facebook/BeaviaCZ |

**Positioning:** Mass-market health food brand. Largest reach and retail distribution.

**Messaging themes:**
- "One of the healthiest foods in the world"
- Digestive health / wellness
- Versatile (side dish, ingredient, standalone)
- Free gift over 800 Kč, free shipping over 1,998 Kč

**Campaign activity:**
- **TV campaign "Tak si mě vem!" (Take Me!)** — launched Jan 15, 2026 as TV sponsorship messages
- First-ever TV presence → signals major investment in brand awareness
- Campaign shows everyday fridge moment — relatable, snacking occasion
- Agency: not disclosed
- Digital ad presence: limited publicly visible Meta/Google ads

**Creative style:** Clean, professional product photography. Mass-market friendly tone. Moving toward emotional/lifestyle from functional.

**Strengths:** Retail distribution moat, BIO line, international expansion, TV budget
**Weaknesses:** Higher free shipping threshold (1,998 Kč), less artisanal positioning, generic health messaging

**Strengths:** Widest SKU range, unique flavors (pumpkin, topinambur, beet kimchi), DIY paste, physical presence, workshops

### 3. Czech Kimchi (czech-kimchi.cz)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Company** | CZECH KIMCHI s.r.o. (IČO 06456642), Bruzovice, northern Moravia. Founded late 2017 by Radek Sojka & Lucie Peterková |
| **Origin** | Inspired by Korean neighbors in their Frýdek apartment building who shared homemade kimchi |
| **Products** | 2 kimchi variants (fish sauce + wakame, soy sauce/vegan), Pickles Klasik, Pickles Orient |
| **Sizes** | 180g, 280g, 450g, 1kg, **5kg, 10kg** (B2B/bulk) |
| **Pricing** | 180g: 75–79 Kč; 280g: 108–117 Kč; 500g: 155–163 Kč; 1kg: 229–239 Kč; 5kg: 1,090–1,140 Kč |
| **Distribution** | D2C eshop (Shoptet), Scuk.cz, B2B trade page for restaurants |
| **Social** | Facebook/CZECH.kimchi (~590 likes), **no Instagram** |
| **Platform** | Shoptet template, seznam.cz email — functional but unpolished |

**Positioning:** Budget-friendly, authentic Korean-style craft producer. Lowest prices in market.

**Messaging themes:**
- "We are not a factory, we are a craft workshop"
- 10 years of fermentation experience
- "100% honest ingredients, zero compromises on taste"
- "From hallway to your table" — humble origin story
- Living fermentation, unpasteurized
- Uses "probiotics" freely (**regulatory risk** — less careful than Živina)

**Campaign activity:**
- **Zero paid advertising** — confirmed in Meta Ad Library (no active or historical campaigns)
- No Google Ads detected
- No content marketing, no blog, no recipes
- Customer acquisition entirely organic: word of mouth, local markets

**Creative style:** Simple, homemade aesthetic. "Garage brand" feel — competent but not premium.

**Strengths:** Lowest prices (30–40% cheaper per gram), authentic fish sauce variant, compelling origin story, bulk B2B sizes (5–10kg), cooled delivery option
**Weaknesses:** ~590 Facebook likes (no Instagram), zero paid media, only 2 kimchi flavors vs. Živina's 6, Shoptet template design, regional perception limits scalability

## Živina's Current Campaign

**"Živina ti kryje záda" (Živina's Got Your Back)**
- **Agency:** Unicorn Army
- **Launch:** Mid-November 2025
- **Channels:** YouTube, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), remarketing
- **Creative approach:** "Punk" aesthetic — authentic, fast production. Real Živina team members + 1 community influencer (not actors)
- **Key insights addressed:**
  - Stress from evening dinner preparation
  - Last-minute cooking for children
  - Desire for quality food without effort
- **Positioning:** Modern, fast, honest way to home cooking
- **Next phase:** Larger nationwide campaign planned Q1 2026
